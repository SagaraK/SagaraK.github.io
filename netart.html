"Untitled", Website
This piece is is meant for the user to experience their own personality and reactions to the idea of obedience in terms of using the internet. The internet, while extremely helpful, seems to dull our observational senses and we often go through media believing everything with little to no doubts. The goal of this website it to understand on a greater level and question the content that is being presented.
