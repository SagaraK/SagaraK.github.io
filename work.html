Kevin Sagara Work About

"Untitled", Website
This piece is is meant for the user to experience their own personality and reactions to the idea of obedience in terms of using the internet. The internet, while extremely helpful, seems to dull our observational senses and we often go through media believing everything with little to no doubts. The goal of this website it to understand on a greater level and question the content that is being presented.

"Silhouettes of Emotions", Digital Video, 2min
This is a commentary about how there are many in society who hide their true emotions, rather than displaying their authentic self. The silhouettes are placed on each side of the pillar because those "fake" emotions are being used to build a character or personality that one uses when in public. The sound of a distorted clock in the background is being used to show how much time we worry about our appearance in public and how that reality just does not feel real or natural.

"Incognito", Digital Video, 4min
This is a commentary on the information overload and hidden identity people have when browsing the internet. We spent countless time looking through our daily websites without really thinking about what the internet is really doing to us. Everyone is hidden behind the computer screen and toxic behavior can ensure behind that anonymity.
