Kevin Sagara Work About

"Silhouettes of Emotions",Digital Video, 2mins
This is a commentary about how there are many in society who hide their true emotions, rather than displaying their authentic self. The silhouettes are placed on each side of the pillar because those "fake" emotions are being used to build a character or personality that one uses when in public. The sound of a distorted clock in the background is being used to show how much time we worry about our appearance in public and how that reality just does not feel real or natural.
