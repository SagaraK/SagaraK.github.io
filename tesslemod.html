Artist Statement

Tesselations is an interesting topic that I have never considered in my art practice. This process of creating highly repeatable shapes and patterns and being able to attach them to different shapes through the use of a similar edge allows for a whole range of possibilities. This process allows for not only group collaboration, but also individual style to resonate within a combined design. What especially stood out of me was the process was pretty much the same within Illustrator as it was in real life through origami design.

For my design in Illustrator, I decided to create a piece that resemebles something you would find in a puzzle set. Puzzles are the prime example of tessalations so I thought it was fitting to replicate that idea.
